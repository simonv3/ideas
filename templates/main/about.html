What is Idea Otter?

Do you have any idea? An inspiration? A plan? A project? An answer to a question? Idea Otter is here to help.

Idea Otter is a light and simple way to quickly store your ideas online. You can keep your ideas to yourself, or unleash them to the rest of the world by publishing them to the global field.

We believe in ideas. No matter how simple, ridiculous, or silly. Why? Because ideas lead to movement, which is what gets us there (you know the place I mean). That's why we set Idea Otter up. We want to enable you to share your ideas, to get feedback on them, and to discover more. This isn't twitter, full of links, pancakes, and idleness. And it's not g+, full of good photos. This is an incubator - and we hope the eggs are just about to crack open and fly.

How does it work?

Whenever you think of something, just use the bookmarklet to jot it down. Other users of the site can than see what it is you thought of, comment on it, and help you expand your ideas. Use tags to make it indexable, if you can. We'll suggest some for you.

We don't want to get in the way of you and your thoughts, so we've made the process as simple as possible. Ideas can come at any moment, and you can't always act on them. What you think of could be the next Facebook, or save the planet. Maybe you don't have that idea right now - no bother - You can browse other users' ideas. If you like something, tell them. If you'd love to work on it, share it.

FAQ

Who are we?

We're a couple of students of web design, free thinking, and inspiration.

Idea Otter started as a personal project of Simon to keep some of his ideas on file. It was also an excuse to make a bookmarklet. Neat. He then told Richard about it, who instantly saw what a great tool it would be if it could just be tweaked like so. We asked Olivia to draw us some otters, and here we are.
